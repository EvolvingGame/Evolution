Objectives:________________________________________

Design your ecosystem to survive for as long as possible


Controls:___________________________________________

Set up Predators, Prey, and Resources throughout the grid


Rules:_______________________________________________

Predators and Prey each act differently depending on how many neighbors they have.

Predators: Blue

if next to a prey, it will immediately kill it and move to that square

0 neighbors - move to the next square

1 neighbor - reproduces and does not move

2 or 3 neighbors - move to the next square

4, 5, or 6 neighbors - die from overpopulation

Prey: Green

if next to a resource, it will immediately take it and move to that square

0 neighbors - do not move

1 neighbor - reproduce and do not move

2, 3, or 4 neighbors - move to the next square

5 or 6 neighbors - die from overpopulation
